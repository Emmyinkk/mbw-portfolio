MBW fashionaddict is a fashion brand that focuses majorly on female wears. Our aim is to empower every woman and help build confidences in themselves. We do this by creating collections that embrace every type of body, makes statments and help you accept who you are. We aim to be your one stop shop for everything wears ranging grom lounge wears, to dresses, formal, casual, bridal robes, aso ebi and so much more.

I am Temiloluwa Esther Fagbemi, 27years old from Ondo state Nigeria. The first born or my family with two siblings. I finished from the department of microbiology in Obafemi Awolowo University. I have always wanted to be a fashion designer but due to the stereotype in Nigeria everyone has to go to uni to get a degree (there’s really no opportunity to study fashion in Uni) so I had to go get a degree first like my parents wanted but bearing in mind they told me they’d support me if after I finish uni I still wanted to do fashion. As soon as I was done, before going for nysc, I started learning how to paddle the machine, how to cut and sew using free hand by a road side tailor, I did that for about 6 months. After my compulsory one year service, I decided to go to a proper fashion school and I ended up in GMYT fashion academy in Lekki, Lagos state. Here’s where I learnt pattern cutting and more advance way of cutting and sewing this was in the year 2020, had to end my program abruptly due to the coronavirus lockdown. By the time lockdown was over, in December I started my own brand (this was when mbw, fashionaddict was born). I launched my first collection and it has been amazing from there. In 2022 I started thinking of furthering my education and gaining more knowledge, this was when I decided to travel outside Nigeria and diversify in my experience. I just finished my masters degree here at the Nottingham Trent University, Nottingham Uk I’m flying colors. My graduating collection was selected as one of few students collection that was exhibited in London.
